Secret Soldiers KPI Portal

Login & Password Reset

Portal URL

https://scrtsoldiers20.streamlit.app

First-Time Login

  1. Open the portal link.
  2. Enter your email and the default password provided to you.
  3. Click Sign in.
If you see “Invalid login credentials,” use Reset Password below.

Reset Password (Anytime)

  1. On the login screen, enter your email.
  2. Click Reset password.
  3. Check your email for the reset link and open it.
  4. In the address bar, replace # with ?, then press Enter.
  5. Set your new password.
Example: .../#access_token=... → .../?access_token=...

Access Rules

  • Soldiers: Submit Content + Leaderboard.
  • Sergeants/Captain: Submit Content + Leaderboard + Sergeant Console.
Need help? Contact your sergeant or the captain.
